Multi-agent AI prediction engine that extracts real-world "seed information" (news, policy drafts, financial signals) to construct high-fidelity parallel digital worlds. Thousands of AI agents with independent personalities, long-term memory, and behavioral logic freely interact and socially evolve. Uses GraphRAG for knowledge graph construction, Zep Cloud for agent memory, and the OASIS simulation engine from CAMEL-AI. Supports any OpenAI SDK-compatible LLM API.
